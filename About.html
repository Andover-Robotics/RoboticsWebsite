About Us

Established in 2009, the Andover Robotics Club is a student-run organization that specializes in the construction of various robots for nationwide competitions. We are located in the town of Andover, Massachusetts. The club is led by a 5 member board of officers, who keep it running behind-the-scenes. ARC participates in two competitions, First Tech Challenge and the NSL Ten80 Racing Challenge. In each competition, students focus on learning programming and building concepts. We previously competed in another competition: Botball.
